A longitudinal panel dataset tracking daily API usage for 400 large language models on OpenRouter, spanning July 2024 to February 2026. 01

The Reasoning Revolution

推理模型的崛起

The most dramatic structural shift in the LLM market over our sample period is the rapid rise of reasoning models — systems that produce extended chain-of-thought before answering. In January 2025, reasoning models accounted for under 1% of OpenRouter API requests. By February 2026, they had captured nearly 60% of all traffic, displacing standard models at an extraordinary pace.

本样本期内 LLM 市场最显著的结构性变化，是推理模型的急速崛起——这类系统在回答前会产生扩展的思维链过程。2025年1月，推理模型仅占 OpenRouter API 请求的不足1%。到2026年2月，其流量占比已接近60%，以惊人的速度取代了传统模型。

Key Finding

Reasoning models went from 0% to nearly 60% of all API traffic in 13 months — a structural regime change unprecedented in speed.

推理模型在13个月内从0%攀升至近60%的API流量占比——这是速度史无前例的结构性范式转变。

02

Creator Market Dynamics

创建者市场格局演变

Google dominates by request volume, powered by its low-cost Gemini Flash family targeting high-throughput applications. Anthropic, despite commanding the highest capability ratings, ranks eighth by request count — a textbook illustration of premium-tier positioning in a competitive market. The February 2025 and December 2025 drops in concentration coincide with DeepSeek's R1 release and the broader commoditization of frontier capabilities.

谷歌凭借面向高吞吐应用的低价 Gemini Flash 系列在请求量上占据主导。Anthropic 尽管能力评分最高，请求量却排名第八——这是竞争市场中高端定位策略的典型体现。2025年2月和12月的集中度下降，分别与 DeepSeek R1 发布以及前沿能力广泛商品化相吻合。

03

Capability, Price, and Adoption

能力、价格与市场采用

Mapping model capability (Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index) against adoption reveals a clear segmentation: high-volume models cluster at the lower end of the capability spectrum with minimal pricing, while frontier models attract far fewer requests despite superior benchmarks. The correlation between intelligence index and log-usage is negative — the market systematically favors cost efficiency over raw capability.

将模型能力（Artificial Analysis 智能指数）与采用率进行映射，揭示出清晰的市场分层：高流量模型集中在能力谱系的低端，定价极低；前沿模型尽管基准测试表现卓越，请求量却少得多。智能指数与对数使用量之间呈负相关——市场系统性地偏好成本效率而非原始能力。

04

Data Collection & Methodology

数据收集与方法说明

The usage panel was assembled from two sources. Primary: 5,167 HTML snapshots from the Wayback Machine archive of openrouter.ai/rankings, covering July 2024 to early 2025, parsed by extracting Next.js RSC payload blocks embedded in the page source. Secondary: the OpenRouter live statistics API (/api/v1/stats/token-panel), which provides daily model-level counts from February 2025 onward. The two series are merged with API data taking precedence in overlapping dates.

使用量面板来自两个数据源。主要来源：从 Wayback Machine 存档的 openrouter.ai/rankings 页面抓取 5,167 个 HTML 快照，覆盖 2024年7月至2025年初，通过提取页面源码中嵌入的 Next.js RSC Payload 数据块进行解析。次要来源：OpenRouter 实时统计 API（/api/v1/stats/token-panel），提供从 2025年2月起的逐日模型级别数据。两组序列取并集，重叠日期以实时数据优先。

Model capabilities and pricing are sourced from Artificial Analysis (artificialanalysis.ai), a neutral benchmarking organization. Cross-database matching of 343 OpenRouter model IDs to 399 Artificial Analysis entries was performed using a deterministic two-step procedure: first a manual mapping dictionary covering systematic naming differences across the two databases, then exact string matching after standardization. Overall match rate: 74.6%.

模型能力与定价数据来源于中立基准测试机构 Artificial Analysis。将 343 个 OpenRouter 模型 ID 与 399 个 Artificial Analysis 条目进行跨库匹配，采用两步确定性规则：首先是涵盖两库系统性命名差异的手动映射字典，然后是标准化后的精确字符串匹配。总体匹配率为74.6%。
