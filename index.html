About

I am Hongrui ZHU, a PhD student in Industrial Economics at UCASS. My research goes beyond traditional econometrics, applying AI and economic theory to address complex business and regulatory challenges in the digital age.

The central question: when markets are governed by algorithms and large language models, what new patterns of competition, pricing, and consumer behavior emerge that classical theory never anticipated? Answering this demands both rigorous economic intuition and executable computational tools.

Work in Progress
Simulating Tacit Collusion Under Algorithmic Pricing
Algorithmic Collusion in Q-Learning Pricing Agents · 算法定价下的默契合谋模拟
Research Question · 研究问题
How do supra-competitive prices arise in markets dominated by reinforcement learning algorithms — without any explicit human coordination?在强化学习算法主导定价的市场中，超竞争价格如何在无任何人为协调的情况下自发涌现？
Methodology · 研究方法
We build a MARL-based oligopoly sandbox and demonstrate that under specific exploration rates and discount factors, pricing algorithms inevitably converge toward tacit collusion.构建基于多智能体强化学习的寡头市场沙箱，证明在特定探索率与折扣因子下，定价算法必然收敛至默契合谋。
Policy Relevance · 政策意义
Provides antitrust regulators (FTC, SAMR) with a quantitative toolkit for detecting and auditing algorithmic collusion in digital markets.为反垄断监管机构（FTC、市监总局）提供可量化的工具包，用于检测和审计数字市场中的算法合谋行为。

Consumer Demand Estimation with Large Language Model Agents
Estimating Consumer Demand via LLM Agents · 基于大语言模型智能体的消费者需求估计
Research Question · 研究问题
Traditional discrete choice models fail to capture consumer preferences when faced with unstructured textual product information. Can LLMs close this gap?传统离散选择模型难以处理非结构化文本产品信息中的消费者偏好。大语言模型能否填补这一空缺？
Methodology · 研究方法
We construct a population of heterogeneous consumer agents powered by GPT-4, feeding product text descriptions to reconstruct demand curves in a zero-shot setting.构建由 GPT-4 驱动的异质性消费者智能体群体，输入产品文本描述，在零样本条件下重建需求曲线。
Applications · 应用场景
Provides e-commerce platforms and dynamic pricing systems with more realistic zero-shot market testing environments, reducing reliance on costly field experiments.为电商平台与动态定价系统提供更真实的零样本市场测试环境，降低对高成本实地实验的依赖。

Parameters · 参数
Firms (n) · 厂商数 2
2 or 3 competing algorithms. More firms → harder to sustain coordination.2或3个竞争算法。厂商越多，维持协调越困难。
Learning Rate (α) · 学习率 0.15
Blends past Q-value (weight 1−α) with new experience (weight α). Paper: 0.15. Lower → cautious; higher → reactive.以权重 (1−α) 保留旧估计、权重 α 吸收新经验。论文取 0.15。偏小→保守稳健，偏大→反应快但噪声大。
Discount Factor (δ) · 折扣因子 0.95
Weight on future profits. High δ → agents value long-run gains, making tacit collusion self-sustaining: deviating sacrifices too much future payoff.对未来利润的权重。δ 越高，智能体越"重视未来"。高 δ 使默契合谋自我维系——偏离当期得益，但损失的未来收益更多。
Economic Intuition
Set parameters and click Run. Early on, agents explore randomly — prices fluctuate widely. As exploration decays (ε → 0), each agent's Q-values converge and prices stabilise. With high δ and few firms, the equilibrium price sits well above the Nash competitive level, constituting tacit collusion with no explicit coordination.
